Instructions

  1. The screen is divided into 2 parts. The loop detector on the left and the kinematic chain visualizer canvas on the right.
  2. To draw a node, click anywhere on the canvas.
  3. To draw an edge between two nodes, drag a line from one node to the other.
  4. To delete a node or an edge, double click on it.
  5. The loop detector shows all loops greater than size 4.
  6. Hover over the detected loops to see the loop in the graph.
